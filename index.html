Meet Your Physician

Dr. Khaled Fareed, MD

Board Certified Urologist Integrative Medicine Sexual Wellness Regenerative Medicine Peptide Therapy

Dr. Fareed is a board-certified urologist and founder of Urowell Integrative Care and Uro Center OC in Huntington Beach, California. With over 15 years of experience, he combines traditional urological expertise with cutting-edge integrative medicine — including regenerative therapies, peptide protocols, sexual wellness treatments, and aesthetic procedures.

His patient-first philosophy centers on treating the whole person, not just the condition. Dr. Fareed is a recognized provider of Alma Duo, FemiLift, CO₂ laser resurfacing, Alma TED hair restoration, and stem cell / exosome therapies.
